Block all negative energy out your life. Surround yourself with positivity and good vibes. Become and peace with your emotional state. You need to realize that NO ONE is never always happy. Nor are they each and every day optimistic or the better versions of themselves. Holding yourself to fair expectations is an essential aspect of self-love. You need to accept that some days are going to be better than others, and that having an off day, or week, or depression time is totally okay. Checking in with your emotional situation often is crucial so that you can understand your moods and learn to embrace them.

Build up your confidence and self esteem

An essential part to being able to self love and care is being able to realize that you can't describe beauty. Beauty is what you see it as. Self-esteem can play a major role in your motivation and success all your life. Low self-esteem may hold you back from being successful at school or at work,because you don't believe that you are capable of success.

What is self esteem?

Self-esteem is an individual's subjective evaluation of their own worth. There are two types of self esteem. There's High and Low. High self-esteem means that you believe in yourself and know that you are a wonderful addition to this universe. You see, you may have a few weaknesses, but you also have a lot of strengths that really shape who you are.Then there's low self esteem, low self-esteem means you don't really believe in yourself. You frequently compare yourself to other people, and feel that you don't match up, which is discouraging. You prefer to concentrate on your weaknesses, rather than focus on your strengths.

Positive/High self-esteem has the following features:

  1. Believing in a set of views and beliefs that are firmly placed And being willing, in the face of resistance to them, to protect or express yourself.
  2. Being open to making choices, trusting your own judgement, even if someone does not approve, and not feeling bad about about those decisions.
  3. Not living in the past or in the future. Just completely living in the moment.
  4. Believing in your ability to get through challenges, respond to mistakes, and ask for help.
  5. Engaging in various activities and hobbies and enjoying them.
  6. Believing you are important and that people are going to enjoy spending time with you.
  7. Regardless of differences in wealth and personal achievement, consider yourself self-worthy and equal to others.

Negative/Low self-esteem is characterized by:

  1. Strong self-criticism tends to create a natural state of self-dissatisfaction. Exaggerating the significance of failures or actions and not being able to gain self-forgiveness.
  2. Feelings of meaninglessness.
  3. General negativity about life and often an inability to enjoy life.
  4. Hostility or irritability, quickly irritated by small items.
  5. Uncertainty because of fear of errors being made.
  6. Excessive willingness to please someone out of fear of displeasing them.

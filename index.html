Introducing Imperfect Perfection

What is self love?

The definition of self love is different for each person. Self-love is important for living well. This affects who you choose for a friend, the picture you portray, and how you deal with the issues in your life. Self-love is not simply a state in which one feels good, it is a state of self-esteem that develops overtime. Self-love is complex, it evolves through habits that mature us. When we behave in ways that expand self-love in us, we tend to embrace our limitations and strengths much more and we start to have less need to justify our failures. To describe self-care, it's essentially the act of physically and mentally taking care of yourself. Self-love means unapologetically enjoying yourself, while self-care is about taking the time to feel good on your skin. Although these two are distinct, both are necessity for a safe, fulfilled lifestyle.
